📚 Education Leader

Cambridge ZCTAs dominate education — 02138 and 02139 both exceed 86% bachelor's attainment, among the highest in Massachusetts.

🚗 The Commute Paradox

Wealthy outer-suburb ZCTAs (Concord, Carlisle, Weston) consistently score lowest on commute — residents trade time for space.

📐 Methodology

Each of five indicators normalised 0–100 within the dataset. Commute score inverted (shorter = better). Composite = equal-weighted average of all five.
